directions

From South Kensington Tube station
Selsey House is at a 2 minutes walking distance from South Kensington Tube station and bus services.
From Heathrow
The nearest Airport is Heathrow. You can take the Piccadilly Line and stop at South Kensington or take Heathrow Express, stop at Paddington, then change to the Circle Line and stop at South Kensington. Also, buses (Airport shuttle) are available which stop at Cromwell Road.
If you need more detailed directions please contact us.
